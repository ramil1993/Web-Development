We pride ourselves in providing small businesses with fast computer repairs & upgrade services, honest answers and value. We understand that you want someone local, right here in ZeroLine TechShop to service your small business computers quickly and professionally. Starting out of a home office in ZeroLine TechShop in 2005, we helped hundreds residential and small business clients with their computer woes.

We found that many small businesses required their own IT department, but lacked the resources or had someone that simply “knew what they were doing” filling the position which often left the business struggling with their IT challenges. Realizing the special need for affordable small business computer repairs and IT services, we now specialize in computer repairs and IT services for small business in ZeroLine TechShop and surrounding areas.

We are flexible in that we can provide you with several different options for the repair of your computer, depending on your wants needs and budget, offering both pro-active and reactive IT services. We can even come right to your home office or business with our mobile on-site service!
